Decomposition-based solution framework. The algorithm starts from feasible SP, BS, and AE service-plan columns and solves a restricted master problem. Dual prices are passed to mode-specific pricing problems to generate negative reduced-cost columns. For N <= 100, full pricing is continued until LP convergence and the reported gap is a Full-CG LP-IP gap. For large-scale instances, a strengthened budgeted-CG protocol with incumbent-column injection is used, and the reported gap is a generated-pool LP-IP gap rather than a complete-column global optimality certificate. The final integer schedule is recovered by solving the IRMP on the generated column pool.
